I'm a postdoctoral scholar in Dr. Bharath Chandrasekaran's SoundBrain Lab at Northwestern University. I'm interested in how various aspects of speech and voice are represented in the brain and how our language experience and cognitive mechanisms influence such representations. 
Prior to coming to Northwestern, I completed my PhD through Harvard SHBT under the mentorship of Dr. Tyler Perrachione, and I received BA in Linguistics, Cognitive Science and Psychology at Rice University.
